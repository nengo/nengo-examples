Examples

These examples show how to use NengoLoihi.
They range from simple examples similar to the
`NengoCore examples <https://www.nengo.ai/nengo/examples.html>`_,
to complex examples classifying images using deep learning.

Requirements
============

- `abr_control <https://github.com/abr/abr_control>`_
- `jupyter <https://docs.jupyter.org/en/latest/install.html>`_
- `matplotlib <https://matplotlib.org/users/installing.html>`_
- `nengo <https://www.nengo.ai/nengo/getting_started.html>`_
- `nengo-dl>=3.0 <https://www.nengo.ai/nengo-dl/installation.html>`_
- `nengo-loihi <https://www.nengo.ai/nengo-loihi/>`_
- `tensorflow>=2.5.1 <https://www.tensorflow.org/install>`_
